How to Play

Click on any colored key with your mouse, or press the corresponding keyboard key (A, S, D, F, G, H, J, K) to play notes!

⌨️ A = C ⌨️ S = D ⌨️ D = E ⌨️ F = F ⌨️ G = G ⌨️ H = A ⌨️ J = B ⌨️ K = C(high)
